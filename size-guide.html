SIZE GUIDE

Home / Size Guide

Finding the perfect fit is essential for comfort and longevity of your DeeReeL Footies shoes. Our sizing guide will help you determine the right size for your feet. If you have any questions or need additional assistance, please don't hesitate to contact our customer service team.

IMPORTANT SIZING NOTES

  • DeeReeL Footies shoes typically run true to size, but different lasts may fit differently.
  • If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up.
  • For wider feet, consider selecting a wider width rather than a larger size.
  • Our shoes are handcrafted and may have slight variations in sizing.
  • All measurements in the charts below are approximate.

HOW TO MEASURE YOUR FOOT

  1. Prepare: Place a piece of paper on a hard, flat surface against a wall. Wear the socks you would typically wear with dress shoes.
  2. Position: Stand on the paper with your heel against the wall. Your weight should be evenly distributed on both feet.
  3. Mark: Mark the longest part of your foot on the paper. This is usually the tip of your big toe, but not always.
  4. Measure: Use a ruler to measure the distance from the wall (heel) to the mark (longest toe) in centimeters.
  5. Repeat: Repeat for your other foot, as most people have slight differences between their feet. Use the measurement from your larger foot.
  6. Find your size: Use the measurement to find your size in our conversion charts above.

Pro Tip:

It's best to measure your feet in the evening when they are slightly larger due to natural swelling throughout the day.

UNDERSTANDING DEEREEL FOOTIES LASTS

A shoe last is a three-dimensional mold that replicates a foot shape, used to construct shoes. Different lasts create different fits and aesthetics. Below are our most popular lasts and their characteristics:

MEN'S LASTS

Inca (80)

Our most classic last. Medium width with a rounded toe. Provides excellent comfort while maintaining an elegant silhouette.

Rain (80216)

Slightly wider than Inca with a more contemporary, rounded toe. Excellent for those seeking additional comfort.

Simpson (80483)

Our most elegant last with a sleek, elongated toe. Narrower fit, ideal for formal occasions.

Oscar (80530)

A versatile last with a slightly chiseled toe. Medium width with excellent balance between comfort and elegance.

WOMEN'S LASTS

Madison (W80)

Our classic women's last. Medium width with a slightly rounded toe. Elegant yet comfortable.

Xenia (W80216)

A contemporary last with a rounded toe and slightly wider fit. Ideal for all-day comfort.

Elena (W80483)

Our most elegant women's last with a sleek, elongated toe. Perfect for formal occasions.

Sofia (W80530)

A versatile last with a slightly almond-shaped toe. Balances comfort and style beautifully.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I know if my shoes fit correctly?

A properly fitting shoe should feel snug but not tight. There should be about a thumb's width of space between your longest toe and the end of the shoe. The widest part of your foot should align with the widest part of the shoe, and your heel should not slip when walking.

Will my DeeReeL Footies shoes stretch over time?

Yes, all leather shoes will stretch slightly with wear, particularly in width. However, they will not stretch significantly in length. If a shoe is painfully tight when new, it's likely the wrong size or width.

What if I'm between sizes?

If you're between sizes, we generally recommend sizing up rather than down. You can always use insoles or tongue pads to adjust the fit of a slightly larger shoe, but a too-small shoe will cause discomfort and cannot be stretched significantly.

How do I choose the right width?

If you have never had issues with standard width shoes, our standard width (D for men, B for women) should work well for you. If you find that shoes are often too tight across the ball of your foot or instep, consider a wider width. If shoes often feel too roomy, a narrower width might be better.

Can I return shoes if they don't fit?

Yes, we accept returns of unworn shoes in their original packaging within 30 days of delivery. Please note that custom or MADE ON ORDER shoes cannot be returned unless there is a manufacturing defect.
